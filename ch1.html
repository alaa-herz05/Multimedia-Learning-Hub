Definition of Multimedia

تعريف الوسائط المتعددة
What is Multimedia? ما هي الوسائط المتعددة؟

Multimedia means that computer information can be represented through audio, video, and animation in addition to traditional media such as text, graphics/drawings, and images.

Arabic الوسائط المتعددة تعني أن معلومات الحاسوب يمكن تمثيلها باستخدام الصوت والفيديو والتحريك، بالإضافة إلى الوسائط التقليدية مثل النصوص والرسومات والصور.
Multimedia — Word Definition تعريف الكلمة
🔤 Multi

Multi means many or more than one.

Multi تعني متعدد أو أكثر من واحد.
📡 Media

Media means the means by which something is communicated, such as sound or television.

Media تعني وسيلة نقل أو توصيل المعلومات إلى الحواس.
Extra Meaning Media can also mean the intervening substance through which impressions are conveyed to the senses, such as light passing from one medium into another.
General Working Definition التعريف العام
Core Definition Multimedia is the field concerned with the computer-controlled integration of text, graphics, drawings, still and moving images (video), animation, audio, and any other media where every type of information can be represented, stored, transmitted, and processed digitally.
بالعربي الوسائط المتعددة هي مجال يهتم بدمج النصوص والرسومات والصور الثابتة والمتحركة والفيديو والتحريك والصوت وأي وسائط أخرى باستخدام الحاسوب، بحيث يمكن تمثيل المعلومات وتخزينها ونقلها ومعالجتها رقمياً.

Future Trends 13.8

الاتجاهات المستقبلية
1. Virtual Environments البيئات الافتراضية

Virtual environments are computer-based systems that are 3D rather than 2D and interactive rather than passive.

Arabic البيئات الافتراضية هي أنظمة حاسوبية ثلاثية الأبعاد وتفاعلية، وليست ثنائية الأبعاد أو سلبية.
🔑 Main Ideas
  • Provide a sense of spatial presence to the user.
  • Use visual, auditory, or tactile feedback.
  • Can include synthesized material such as computer graphics.
  • Can include recorded material such as video.
  • Can involve the surrounding real world through augmented reality.
🕶️ Devices
  • Head-tracked displays.
  • Stereo displays for visual and audio output.
  • Hand trackers.
  • Haptic displays for touch and temperature.
Exam Note Virtual Environments = 3D + Interactive + Spatial Presence + Devices such as head trackers, hand trackers, and haptic displays.
2. Ubiquitous Computing الحوسبة في كل مكان

Ubiquitous computing is an environment in which a large number of computers are seamlessly integrated into our immediate surroundings and connected by wireless networks.

Arabic الحوسبة في كل مكان تعني وجود عدد كبير من الحواسيب مدمجة بسلاسة في البيئة المحيطة بنا، ومتصلة من خلال شبكات لاسلكية.
🔑 Key Features
  • Users are no longer aware of the computers' presence.
  • People and computers are tracked as they move.
  • Computers adapt transparently to the user's preferences.
⚙️ Main Components
  • Inexpensive, low-power, mobile computers and displays.
  • Software infrastructure for ubiquitous applications.
  • High-capacity wireless networks that tie everything together.
Analogy The idea is similar to the electric motor: it exists in many devices, but users usually do not notice it directly.
