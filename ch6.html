Introduction to Sound

مقدمة في الصوت
Acoustics · Decibels · Energy · Harmonics
🔊 What is Sound? ما هو الصوت؟
  • Sound is created by vibrations in the air that produce waves of pressure perceived by the ear.
  • Sound waves vary in sound pressure level (amplitude) and in frequency / pitch.
  • Acoustics is the branch of physics that studies sound.
  • Sound pressure level (loudness/volume) is measured in decibels (dB).
  • Sound is energy — too much volume can permanently damage your ears.
  • The perception of loudness depends on the frequency or pitch.
  • Harmonics cause the same note played on a cello to sound different from the same note on a piano — the waveform shape differs.
شرح بالعربي الصوت ناتج عن اهتزازات في الهواء تُولّد موجات ضغط تُدرَك كصوت. يُقاس الصوت بـ الديسيبل (dB). علم الصوتيات Acoustics هو فرع الفيزياء الذي يدرس الصوت. الصوت هو طاقة — الأصوات العالية جداً تُلحق ضرراً دائماً بالسمع. التوافقيات (Harmonics) هي ما تجعل نفس النوتة تُصدر صوتاً مختلفاً على آلات مختلفة.

System Sounds

أصوات النظام
Mac & Windows · .WAV · Built-in Recorders
🖥️ System & OS Sounds
🍎 Macintosh
  • Built-in sound recorder included.
  • System sounds: glass, indigo, laugh.
  • Audio stored as AIFF / AIFC format.
🪟 Windows
  • Built-in sound recorder included.
  • Sounds: start.wav, chimes.wav, chord.wav.
  • Stored as .WAV in Windows\Media directory.

System sounds are assigned to events like startup, warnings, and errors. You can add your own sounds by placing them in the Windows\Media directory and selecting them from the Sound Control Panel. MS Office includes additional sound files as well.

شرح بالعربي كلٌّ من Mac و Windows يحتوي على مسجّل صوت مدمج. أصوات Windows مخزّنة بصيغة .WAV في مجلد Windows\Media. أصوات Mac بصيغة AIFF/AIFC. يمكن إضافة أصوات مخصّصة وتحديدها من لوحة تحكم الصوت.

MIDI Audio

صوت MIDI
Musical Instrument Digital Interface · How it works · Pros & Cons
🎹 What is MIDI?
  • MIDI = Musical Instrument Digital Interface — a communications standard developed in the 1980s for electronic instruments and computers.
  • Allows instruments from different manufacturers to communicate with each other.
  • MIDI data is NOT digitized sound — it is music stored in numeric/instruction format (like a musical score).
  • Quality depends on the end user's playback device — MIDI is device dependent.
  • A MIDI file is a list of commands: recordings of musical actions sent to a MIDI player to produce sound.
  • MIDI can synthesize over 100 instruments.
  • MIDI is not easily used to reproduce spoken dialog/speech.
  • Length of MIDI file can be changed without affecting pitch — just vary the tempo.
شرح بالعربي MIDI = واجهة الآلات الموسيقية الرقمية. طُوِّرت في الثمانينيات. ليست تسجيلاً للصوت — بل هي تعليمات رقمية (مثل ورقة نوتات موسيقية). جودتها تعتمد على جهاز التشغيل (Device Dependent). تستطيع محاكاة أكثر من 100 آلة. لا تصلح بسهولة للكلام المنطوق. يمكن تغيير طولها دون التأثير على درجة الصوت.
🛠️ Creating MIDI — What You Need
Required Hardware
  • Sound Synthesizer — built into PC sound board; add-on for Mac.
  • MIDI Keyboard or other MIDI device.
Required Software & Skills
  • Sequencer Software (e.g. Smart Score).
  • Knowledge of music theory.
  • Ability to play piano or hire an expert.
شرح بالعربي لإنشاء MIDI تحتاج: برنامج Sequencer (مثل Smart Score) + مُولِّد صوت (Synthesizer) مدمج في كرت الصوت (للكمبيوتر) أو إضافة خارجية (للـ Mac) + لوحة مفاتيح MIDI + معرفة نظرية الموسيقى.
🌐 MIDI on the Web
  • MIDI files are very small — load and play quickly on the web.
  • Two ways to play MIDI on web: (1) Download then play — wait for full download. (2) Streaming — buffer and play while downloading.
  • FLASH allows MIDI/sound integration in multimedia, controlled by buttons, saved as .mp3.

Digital Audio

الصوت الرقمي
Sampling · Bit Resolution · Quantization · CD Standard
💿 How Digital Audio Works
  • Digital audio is a representation of the original sound stored as thousands of numbers (samples) — like a bitmap image.
  • Each number represents the loudness at a discrete slice of time.
  • Digital audio is NOT device dependent — it sounds the same on every device.
  • Sampling rate is measured in kilohertz (kHz). Higher rate = better quality = larger file.
  • Quantization = rounding each sample value to the nearest integer during digitization. Introduces small error, but necessary for digital storage.
  • Used for music CDs — device independent.
شرح بالعربي الصوت الرقمي نسخة من الصوت الأصلي مُخزَّنة كأرقام (عيّنات). كل رقم يمثّل الشدة الصوتية في لحظة زمنية. مستقل عن الجهاز (Device Independent) — يُصدر نفس الصوت على أي جهاز. التكميم (Quantization) = تقريب قيمة كل عيّنة لأقرب عدد صحيح.
📐 Sampling Rates & Bit Resolution
Sampling RateQuality LevelUse Case
44.1 kHzCD QualityCD-quality audio
22.05 kHzGood QualityMultimedia, web audio
11.025 kHzBasic / SpeechVoice, narration
Bit Resolution
  • 8-bit — basic quality
  • 16-bit — CD quality
  • More bits = finer detail in amplitude
Mono vs Stereo
  • Mono = 1 channel, flat sound
  • Stereo = 2 channels, realistic
  • Stereo uses 2× the storage of Mono
شرح بالعربي ثلاث ترددات عيّنات شائعة: 44.1 كيلوهرتز (جودة CD) | 22.05 كيلوهرتز (جودة جيدة) | 11.025 كيلوهرتز (أساسي/كلام). دقة البت: 8-bit أساسي، 16-bit جودة CD. الستيريو يستهلك ضعف مساحة المونو.
🔢 File Size Formula
File Size Calculation File Size (bytes) = Sampling Rate × Duration (sec) × (Bit Resolution ÷ 8) × Tracks
Tracks: 1 = Mono, 2 = Stereo
📘 File Size Example 44,100 Hz × 11 sec × (16÷8) × 1 (Mono) = 970,200 bytes ≈ 0.97 MB
Stereo: 44,100 × 11 × 2 × 2 = 1,940,400 bytes ≈ 1.94 MB
CD-quality audio uses 16-bit sample size and 44.1 kHz sample rate.
شرح بالعربي معادلة حجم الملف: معدل العيّنات × المدة (ثانية) × (دقة البت ÷ 8) × عدد القنوات
مثال: 44100 × 10 × (16÷8) × 2 (ستيريو) = 1,764,000 بايت ≈ 1.68 MB

MIDI vs Digital Audio

MIDI مقابل الصوت الرقمي
Analogy · Pros · Cons · When to use each
⚖️ The Core Analogy — Like Graphics!
🎹 MIDI ← like → Vector Graphics
  • Stores instructions/numeric data to recreate sound
  • Device dependent — different sound on different devices
  • Very small file size
  • Can change length without changing pitch
💿 Digital Audio ← like → Bitmap Image
  • Stores actual sampled waveform (a copy)
  • Device independent — same sound everywhere
  • Larger file size
  • Quality depends on sampling quality at recording time
شرح بالعربي تمثيل بياني: MIDI = رسومات المتجهات (Vector) — تخزّن التعليمات لإعادة الرسم. الصوت الرقمي = رسومات النقطية (Bitmap) — تخزّن نسخة من الصوت الفعلي. MIDI معتمد على الجهاز، الرقمي مستقل عنه.
📋 Full Comparison Table
FeatureMIDIDigital Audio
NatureInstructions / ScoreSampled waveform copy
Device DependencyDependent 🔴Independent ✅
File SizeVery small ✅Large 🔴
Web LoadingFast ✅Slower 🔴
Speech/Dialog❌ Not suitable✅ Perfect
ConsistencyInconsistent 🔴Consistent ✅
Music TheoryRequired 🔴Not required ✅
Change LengthWithout pitch change ✅Affects quality 🔴
Instruments100+ synthesized ✅Records actual sound
🎯 When to Use Each?
✅ Use MIDI When…
  • You don't have enough RAM/bandwidth for digital audio.
  • You have a high-quality sound source.
  • You have complete control over the playback hardware.
  • You do NOT need spoken dialog.
✅ Use Digital Audio When…
  • You do NOT have control over the playback hardware.
  • You have computing resources and bandwidth for larger files.
  • You need spoken dialog.
  • You need consistent quality across all devices.
شرح بالعربي استخدم MIDI إذا: كانت الموارد محدودة، تتحكم في جهاز التشغيل، لا تحتاج كلاماً منطوقاً.
استخدم الصوت الرقمي إذا: لا تتحكم في الجهاز، تحتاج كلاماً منطوقاً، تريد جودة متسقة.

Audio Production

نصائح الإنتاج
Recording · File Size vs Quality · Levels · Editing · Timing
🎬 Planning Your Audio Production
1
Decide what sounds you need and include them in the storyboard or cue sheet.
2
Decide whether to use MIDI or digital audio.
3
Acquire source material — record it yourself or purchase it.
4
Edit the sounds — trim, splice, normalize.
5
Test the sounds to ensure they are timed properly with the multimedia project.
شرح بالعربي خطوات إنتاج الصوت: (1) حدد الأصوات في القصة المصوّرة. (2) اختر MIDI أو رقمي. (3) احصل على المصدر. (4) حرّره. (5) اختبره مع المشروع.
🎙️ Recording from Analog Sources
  • You can digitize sound from a microphone, synthesizer, tape recording, TV broadcast, or CD.
  • To prepare digital audio from analog media (e.g. a tape recorder), record it directly into your computer using digitizing software.
  • Digitized sound is sampled every nth of a second — the more frequent the sampling, the better the sound quality.
  • Sample sizes are either 8 or 16 bits, and common frequencies are 11.025, 22.05, and 44.1 kHz.
  • Balance sound quality with available RAM — higher quality means larger files.
  • Set proper recording levels for a clear, undistorted recording — too low gives noise, too high causes clipping.
  • After recording, edit the sound: trim silence, splice segments, and normalize volume levels.
شرح بالعربي يمكن رقمنة الصوت من: ميكروفون، مُركِّب صوت (Synthesizer)، تسجيل كاسيت، بث تلفزيوني، أو CD. لتحويل الصوت التناظري (Analog) إلى رقمي، سجّله من الجهاز مباشرةً إلى الكمبيوتر باستخدام برنامج رقمنة. وازن بين جودة الصوت وحجم الذاكرة المتاحة. اضبط مستويات التسجيل بشكل صحيح لتجنب التشويش أو القطع. بعد التسجيل حرّر الصوت: اقطع الصمت، ورتّب الأجزاء، وعدّل مستوى الصوت.
🌐 Playing Audio on the Web & Streaming
📥 Method 1 — Download then Play
  • Wait for the entire file to download before playback begins.
  • Uses a helper application to play the file after download.
  • Reliable but causes a delay for the user.
📡 Method 2 — Streaming
  • File is stored in a buffer and played while still downloading.
  • Streaming is dependent on connection speed — slow connections may cause interruptions.
  • MIDI files are preferred for web because they are very small and load fast.
💡 FLASH Note FLASH allows sound to be integrated into a multimedia presentation, controlled by interactive buttons, and saved as .mp3 files — enabling both background music and interactive audio.
شرح بالعربي طريقتان لتشغيل الصوت على الويب:
(1) التنزيل ثم التشغيل: انتظر حتى يكتمل تنزيل الملف ثم يُشغَّل بتطبيق مساعد.
(2) البث (Streaming): يُخزَّن في Buffer ويُشغَّل أثناء التنزيل — يعتمد على سرعة الاتصال.
FLASH يسمح بدمج الصوت في العروض التفاعلية وحفظه بصيغة .mp3.

Exam Questions & Answers

أسئلة متوقعة وإجاباتها
Expected exam questions with model answers
Q 01 What is sound, and how is it measured? ▶ ANSWER Sound is energy created by vibrations in the air that produce waves of pressure. Sound pressure level (loudness) is measured in decibels (dB). The study of sound is called Acoustics. Sound waves vary in amplitude (volume) and frequency (pitch).
Q 02 What are the four frequency ranges? Give the range for each. ▶ ANSWER Infrasound: 0–20 Hz | Human Hearing: 20 Hz–20 kHz | Ultrasound: 20 kHz–1 GHz | Hypersound: 1 GHz–10 THz
Q 03 What is MIDI? Is it digitized sound? What are its main characteristics? ▶ ANSWER MIDI (Musical Instrument Digital Interface) is a communications standard developed in the 1980s. It is NOT digitized sound — it stores music as numeric instructions (like a score). It is device dependent, produces very small files, can synthesize 100+ instruments, and cannot easily reproduce spoken dialog. Length can be changed without affecting pitch.
Q 04 What do you need to create MIDI audio? ▶ ANSWER You need: (1) Sequencer software (e.g. Smart Score), (2) Sound synthesizer — built into PC sound board or an add-on for Mac, (3) MIDI keyboard or device, (4) knowledge of music theory, and (5) ability to play piano or hire an expert.
Q 05 What is digital audio? How is it different from MIDI? ▶ ANSWER Digital audio is a representation of the original sound stored as thousands of numbers (samples). Unlike MIDI, it records actual sound waves. It is device independent (same sound on all devices), used for music CDs, and suitable for speech. It has larger file sizes than MIDI and requires no music theory knowledge to create.
Q 06 What is quantization in digital audio? ▶ ANSWER Quantization is the process of rounding each sample value to the nearest integer during digitization. It introduces a small rounding error but is necessary to store analog sound as digital numbers.
Q 07 What are the three most common sampling rates used in multimedia? Which is CD quality? ▶ ANSWER The three most common rates are: 44.1 kHz (CD quality), 22.05 kHz (good/multimedia quality), and 11.025 kHz (basic/speech). 44.1 kHz is the CD standard.
Q 08 How do you calculate the file size of a digital audio recording? ▶ ANSWER File Size (bytes) = Sampling Rate × Duration (seconds) × (Bit Resolution ÷ 8) × Channels
Example — 10 sec, 44.1 kHz, 16-bit, stereo: 44,100 × 10 × 2 × 2 = 1,764,000 bytes ≈ 1.68 MB
Q 09 Compare MIDI and Digital Audio — give at least 4 differences. ▶ ANSWER MIDI: Instructions/score format | Device dependent | Very small files | Cannot reproduce speech | Requires music theory | Change length without pitch change.
Digital Audio: Sampled waveform copy | Device independent | Large files | Perfect for speech | No music theory needed | Consistent quality everywhere.
Q 10 When should you use MIDI vs Digital Audio? ▶ ANSWER Use MIDI when: RAM/bandwidth is limited, you control the playback hardware, you have a high-quality sound source, and you don't need spoken dialog.
Use Digital Audio when: you don't control playback hardware, you need speech/dialog, you have computing resources for large files, and you need consistent quality across devices.
Q 11 What are the two ways to play audio on the web? What is streaming? ▶ ANSWER (1) Download then play: wait for the entire file to download, then play it with a helper application.
(2) Streaming: the file is stored in a buffer and played while still downloading. Streaming depends on connection speed.
Q 12 What audio formats does Windows use? What about Macintosh? What format works on both? ▶ ANSWER Windows: .WAV files stored in the Windows\Media directory.
Macintosh: AIFF / AIFC files.
Both platforms support .MID / .MIDI (MIDI) files and .MP3.
